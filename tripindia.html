Incredible India: A 15-Day Discovery

Embark on a journey through the heart of India, from ancient monuments and spiritual ghats to bustling cities and serene backwaters.

The Journey of a Lifetime

This 15-day itinerary is carefully crafted to offer you a comprehensive experience of India's diverse culture, history, and natural beauty. You will traverse the famous Golden Triangle, witness spiritual rituals on the Ganges, feel the pulse of Mumbai, and relax on the sun-kissed beaches of Goa before finding tranquility in the backwaters of Kerala.

Detailed 15-Day Itinerary

Days 1-2: Arrival & Exploring Delhi

Arrive in Delhi, India's capital. Settle in and prepare for an exploration of Old and New Delhi. Visit the majestic Red Fort, the sprawling Jama Masjid mosque, and the historic Qutub Minar. Experience a rickshaw ride through the bustling lanes of Chandni Chowk and pay respects at Raj Ghat, Mahatma Gandhi's memorial.

Days 3-4: The Iconic Taj Mahal

A scenic drive takes you to Agra. Witness the breathtaking beauty of the Taj Mahal at sunrise, a white marble mausoleum that is a testament to eternal love. Later, explore the imposing Agra Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage site that served as the main residence of Mughal emperors.

Days 5-6: The Pink City of Jaipur

Travel to Jaipur, stopping at the perfectly preserved ghost city of Fatehpur Sikri en route. In Jaipur, the "Pink City," visit the magnificent Amber Fort, enjoying an elephant ride. Explore the City Palace, the Jantar Mantar observatory, and the iconic Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds).

Days 7-8: Spiritual Varanasi

Take a flight to the holy city of Varanasi. Experience a boat ride on the sacred Ganges River at dawn, witnessing ancient rituals and cremations at the ghats. In the evening, be mesmerized by the Ganga Aarti ceremony, a spectacle of light, sound, and devotion. Also, take a short trip to Sarnath, where Buddha gave his first sermon.

Days 9-10: Bustling Mumbai

Fly to Mumbai, the vibrant financial capital and home of Bollywood. Visit the Gateway of India, drive along Marine Drive, and explore the bustling Crawford Market. Take a ferry to Elephanta Caves to see ancient rock-cut temples. Get a glimpse into the world of Indian cinema with a tour of a film city.

Days 11-12: Sun-Kissed Goa

A short flight takes you to Goa, India's beach paradise. Spend your days relaxing on sandy shores like Baga and Anjuna. Explore the Portuguese-influenced architecture of Old Goa, including the Basilica of Bom Jesus. Enjoy fresh seafood and the vibrant nightlife.

Days 13-14: Tranquil Kerala

Fly to Kochi (Cochin) in Kerala, "God's Own Country". Explore the historic Fort Kochi area with its Chinese fishing nets. The highlight is an overnight stay on a traditional houseboat, gliding through the serene backwaters of Alleppey, surrounded by lush paddy fields and quaint villages.

Day 15: Departure

Enjoy a final Keralan breakfast before heading to the Kochi airport for your departure, filled with unforgettable memories of your incredible journey through India.
