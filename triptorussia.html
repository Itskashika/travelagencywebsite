Russian Escape: A 7-Day Cultural Tour

From grand palaces to iconic cathedrals, experience the history and soul of Russia in just one week

Explore the Russian Wonders

This 7-day itinerary covers the cultural richness of Moscow, the imperial beauty of St. Petersburg, and the serenity of countryside landscapes, offering a perfect blend of history, art, and Russian soul.

Detailed 7-Day Itinerary

Welcome to Moscow – History, Heritage & Hidden Gems

Begin your journey in Moscow, where grand cathedrals, Soviet architecture, and vibrant streets await. Explore iconic sights like the Kremlin, St. Basil’s Cathedral, and the opulent metro, while enjoying rich culture, hearty cuisine, and the city’s timeless charm.

Days 3–4: St. Petersburg – Art, Canals & Imperial Splendor

Travel to St. Petersburg, Russia’s cultural capital, known for its baroque beauty and canal-lined streets. Discover masterpieces at the Hermitage, visit majestic cathedrals, and experience Russia’s imperial past while strolling along the elegant Nevsky Prospect. .

Days 5–6: Royal Escapes – Peterhof & Catherine Palace

Step into the luxurious world of the Russian czars with day trips to the majestic Peterhof Palace and Catherine Palace. Admire golden fountains, manicured gardens, and the famous Amber Room, capturing the grandeur of Russia’s royal heritage.

Day 7: Farewell & Reflections

Enjoy a relaxed final morning in St. Petersburg, picking up souvenirs and soaking in scenic beauty before departure. Reflect on your journey through Russia’s rich history, opulent landmarks, and unforgettable cultural encounters as your adventure concludes.
