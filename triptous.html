American Discovery: A 10-Day Tour

Experience the best of the American West, from iconic cities to breathtaking national parks, on this unforgettable 10-day journey.

A West Coast Adventure

This 10-day itinerary is designed to showcase the stunning diversity of the American West. You'll explore the vibrant streets of San Francisco, stand in awe of Yosemite's grandeur, experience the dazzling lights of Las Vegas, marvel at the Grand Canyon, and soak up the glamour of Los Angeles.

Detailed 10-Day Itinerary

Days 1-2: Iconic San Francisco

Arrive in San Francisco and check into your hotel. Spend the next two days exploring the "City by the Bay." Ride a cable car, see the Golden Gate Bridge, visit the infamous Alcatraz Island, and enjoy the sights and sounds of Fisherman's Wharf.

Days 3-4: Majestic Yosemite

Pick up your rental car and take a scenic drive to Yosemite National Park. Marvel at towering granite cliffs like El Capitan and Half Dome, see stunning waterfalls, and walk among giant sequoia trees. Enjoy hikes for all skill levels and soak in the natural beauty.

Days 5-6: Dazzling Las Vegas

Drive through the desert to Las Vegas, the entertainment capital of the world. Check into your hotel on the famous Strip, catch a world-class show, try your luck at the casinos, and enjoy the incredible dining and nightlife options this city has to offer.

Days 7-8: Awe-Inspiring Grand Canyon

Take a day trip or an overnight stay at the Grand Canyon National Park. Walk along the South Rim for breathtaking panoramic views of one of the world's natural wonders. Watch a spectacular sunrise or sunset over the canyon for a truly unforgettable experience.

Days 9-10: Glamorous LA & Departure

Drive to Los Angeles. Explore the glamour of Hollywood, walk the Walk of Fame, see the mansions of Beverly Hills, and relax on the beach at Santa Monica Pier. On day 10, enjoy a final American breakfast before heading to LAX for your departure.
