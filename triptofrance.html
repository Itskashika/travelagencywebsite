Enchanting France: A 10-Day Cultural & Scenic Odyssey

From Parisian elegance to Riviera glamour, explore the diverse beauty, heritage, and flavors of France in this unforgettable 10-day journey.

A Journey Through France: Culture, Cuisine & Charm

Discover the best of France in 10 days—explore iconic Paris, charming villages, royal palaces, scenic countryside, and the stunning Riviera. A perfect blend of culture, history, and cuisine awaits on this unforgettable journey through the heart of France.

Detailed 10-Day Itinerary

Days 1–2: Paris – Romance, Art & Iconic Landmarks

Begin your journey in Paris, the timeless heart of France. Climb the Eiffel Tower, stroll along the Seine, and admire masterpieces in the Louvre. Discover Notre-Dame, charming Montmartre, and sip coffee at street cafés that breathe history, elegance, and Parisian spirit.

Days 3–4: Versailles & Loire Valley – Royal Grandeur & Castles

Visit the grandeur of Versailles with its Hall of Mirrors and lush gardens. Then venture to the Loire Valley to explore magnificent Renaissance castles like Chambord and Chenonceau. Enjoy local wines and countryside charm in a region rich with royal legacy.

Days 5–6: Lyon – Gastronomy & Historic Charm

Head to Lyon, France’s culinary capital, where cobbled streets meet world-class cuisine. Explore the UNESCO-listed old town, the Basilica of Notre-Dame de Fourvière, and lively food markets. Taste authentic Lyonnaise dishes in traditional bouchons for an unforgettable gourmet experience.

Days 7–8: Provence – Lavender Fields & Hilltop Villages

Soak up the serene beauty of Provence. Wander through lavender fields, explore the ochre cliffs of Roussillon, and stroll the medieval streets of Gordes. Visit Avignon’s Papal Palace and savor rosé wines under the golden southern sun of rural France.

Days 9–10: French Riviera – Glamour, Beaches & Blue Waters

Conclude your trip on the sparkling Côte d’Azur. Bask on Nice’s beaches, explore old town markets, and marvel at Monaco’s luxury. Enjoy clifftop villages like Èze, Mediterranean cuisine, and sunset strolls along the Promenade des Anglais for a stylish finale.
